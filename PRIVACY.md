# Privacy Policy — Lioa

**Lioa does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data.**

Everything happens locally in your browser. The extension reads the LinkedIn
feed page's DOM to determine each post author's connection degree and hides the
posts that aren't from your 1st-degree connections. It makes **no network
requests of its own**, contacts **no servers**, and uses **no analytics or
tracking** of any kind.

The only thing it stores is your on/off toggle preference, kept in your
browser's local `localStorage`. That value never leaves your device.

Lioa is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by LinkedIn.

Questions or issues: https://github.com/DominicJamesWhite/LinkedInOhneAlgo/issues
